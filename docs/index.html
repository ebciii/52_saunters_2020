Bryan Crenshaw’s 52 Saunters Challenge 2020

Inspired by the 52 Hike Challenge, I am taking my own approach, by going on a “saunter” on a different trail averaging one for every week of 2020. These hikes will be summarized in a table below with links to the route on tracking software sites, in case you are interested in taking your own saunter along those trails. If you are curious about why I use the term saunter, instead of hike, it comes from a quote by John Muir, who also preferred the term saunter (see the About page for details).
